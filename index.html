Don't worry, the app isn't broken! Since I'm an indie developer and don't pay Apple $99/year for a certificate, macOS Gatekeeper flags the app as "unsigned."

How to authorize it in 10 seconds:

  1. Open your Terminal app.
  2. Paste this command: xattr -cr "/Applications/LaTeX & BibTeX Tools.app"
  3. Hit Enter. That's it!
